Our Courses

COURSE WE OFFER

Certainly! Heres a concise overview of the courses offered:

BCA

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications): Content: Covers programming languages, software engineering, database management, and web development. Features: Emphasizes practical skills and hands-on experience, with opportunities for internships and industry projects.

BA

BA (Bachelor of Arts): Content: Offers diverse fields such as humanities, social sciences, and languages. Features: Flexible curriculum with a focus on critical thinking, communication skills, and analytical abilities.

B.ED

B.Ed (Bachelor of Education): Content: Educational theory, teaching methods, classroom management, and curriculum development. Features: Prepares students for teaching careers with practical teaching experience and pedagogical skills.

MA

MA (Master of Arts): Content: Advanced study in specialized areas such as literature, history, or political science. Features: Provides in-depth knowledge, research opportunities, and preparation for academic or professional careers.

BTC

BTC (Basic Training Certificate): Content: Practical training in specific vocational or technical skills. Features: Short-term, intensive courses designed to enhance employability and provide foundational skills in various trades.

D.EL.ED

D.El.Ed (Diploma in Elementary Education): Content: Fundamentals of early childhood education, teaching strategies, and educational psychology. Features: Focuses on training for teaching at the elementary level with hands-on teaching experience and pedagogical knowledge.

Our Facilities

Our college offers a comprehensive range of facilities designed to enhance student life and academic success. The library provides a quiet and resource-rich environment for study and research, equipped with an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources. For sports enthusiasts, the games facilities include indoor and outdoor options, allowing students to engage in physical activities and sports. The common room serves as a social hub, offering a comfortable space for relaxation, group discussions, and leisure activities. The computer lab is well-equipped with modern computers and software, supporting students in their academic work, projects, and research. Additionally, the campus is equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi, ensuring seamless internet access across all areas, enabling students to stay connected, access online resources, and collaborate effectively. These facilities collectively contribute to a vibrant and supportive academic environment
